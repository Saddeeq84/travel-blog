Visit Mambilla

Mambilla Plateau, located in Taraba State, Nigeria, is a breathtaking highland
region known for its cool climate, lush green landscapes, and towering hills.
Sitting at over 1,600 meters above sea level, it’s the highest plateau in Nigeria, offering
panoramic views, unique biodiversity, and rich cultural heritage.
Famous for attractions like Gashaka-Gumti National Park, Chappal Waddi (Nigeria's highest peak),
and sprawling tea plantations, Mambilla is a haven for nature lovers, hikers, and
those looking to experience Nigeria's serene countryside. With its misty mornings and
scenic vistas, it’s a hidden gem waiting to be explored.
